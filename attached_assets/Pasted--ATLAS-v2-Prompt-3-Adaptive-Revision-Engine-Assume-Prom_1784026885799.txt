# ATLAS v2 — Prompt 3
## Adaptive Revision Engine

Assume Prompt 1 (Content Progress) and Prompt 2 (Timeline) are already implemented.

Implement an adaptive spaced repetition system at the SYSTEM level.

---

## Initial Evaluation

When both Content and QBank are completed, ask:

How well do you know this system?

🟢 Strong
🟡 Average
🔴 Weak

Show this only once after initial completion.

---

## Spaced Repetition

Initial intervals:

Weak = 7 days

Average = 14 days

Strong = 30 days

Multipliers:

Strong = ×2.0

Average = ×1.5

Weak = ×0.5

Minimum interval = 3 days

Maximum interval = 90 days

After every revision:

newInterval = previousInterval × multiplier

Clamp the interval between 3 and 90 days.

nextRevisionDate = today + newInterval

Keep intervals and multipliers configurable.

---

## Revision Workflow

Whenever a revision becomes due, show:

Revision

When completed:

• Increment Revisions Completed

• Update Last Revised

• Ask again:

How well do you know this system?

🟢 Strong

🟡 Average

🔴 Weak

• Update confidence

• Calculate the next interval

• Schedule the next revision

Repeat indefinitely.

---

## Timeline

Every scheduled revision should automatically appear under **Upcoming** in the correct month.

If a revision is not completed on time, automatically move it to **Overdue**.

Display:

Cardiology Revision

4 days overdue

When completed:

• Remove the old Timeline entry

• Schedule the next revision

• Automatically create the next Upcoming entry

Timeline should always stay synchronized with the revision engine.

---

## Today

Automatically display:

• Due revisions

• Overdue revisions

Only actionable revisions should appear.

Completed revisions should disappear immediately.
